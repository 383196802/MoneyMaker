About Us

This is a course rating and review database platform, especially for the University of Toronto.

Key features of our platform include:

A detailed page for each course in the university, where you can view and contribute rating (from 1 star to 5 stars) for the course. You can also browse, post, and rate (like / dislike) comments for the course. The overall rating is calculated and displayed based on the individual ratings.

The page also displays other general information for the course, which allows the platform to not only be a review website, but also a general course information database.

Viewing the information will not require you to login. However, you will need to log in to post comments and ratings; you can easily log in using Facebook or Google accounts.
